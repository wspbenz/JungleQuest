Jungle Quest is an addictive online clicker game where every tap takes you deeper into the wild adventure. Click to earn XP, level up your explorer, and unlock exciting rewards as you progress through the jungle. The more you click, the stronger you become—discover hidden bonuses, climb levels, and master the quest to become the ultimate jungle explorer!<br><br>

## How to Play ⁉️
Since this game is a Web game, That means there's no need to Download the game. <br>You can just visit the website and play the game!
- Visit <a href="https://wspbenz.github.io/JungleQuest/">JungleQuest Website</a><br>
- Start Clicking on the Chest.<br>
- Earn XP and Level UP!<br>
- Earn rewards, Unlock crates and Themes.
